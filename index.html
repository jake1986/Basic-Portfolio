About Me

My name is Jake Rhoads. I have been a middle school Spanish teacher for the past 3 years, however I have decided to leave that career path to pursue something in the technology field. I love to learn languages. Beyond Spanish, I speak Portuguese and I am learning Italian. I decided to learn Portuguese because it is so similar to Spanish. I also had a brief stay in Brazil where I taught English and continued to improve my Portuguese.

I have a brother named Daniel who lives in Spain with his wife Silvia. They live in the city of Barcelona. Silvia is Italian and is from Rome. That is one of the reasons why I decided to learn Italian. My brother is becoming very proficient in Italian and I would like to be able to acquire the same skill. We really enjoy to spend time together. During the summer of 2019 I stayed in Spain for 2 months and Dan, Silvia, and I traveled to Germany together for 9 days. We visited Munich and Berlin. It was an unforgettable experience for all 3 of us.
